## 🎼 About the Genre

Dark country, gothic americana, and outlaw blues are American roots music traditions that explore darker themes — death, sin, loss, and the hard edges of rural American life. Artists like Johnny Cash, Townes Van Zandt, 16 Horsepower, and Nick Cave helped define this sound. Dark Country Boy carries this tradition forward with 70 albums spanning dark country, folk-noir, outlaw blues, and gothic americana.
